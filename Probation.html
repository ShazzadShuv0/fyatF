warning_amber Academic Probation

When You're Placed on Probation

Probation Triggers
  • Overall CGPA falls below 2.00.
  • If GPA of any Semester falls below 2.00 and your overall CGPA is greater than 2.00, you won't be in probation.
  • If GPA of any Semester falls below 2.00 and your overall CGPA is less than 2.00, you will be in probation.
Probation Restrictions
  • Maximum 6 credit hours per semester.
  • Have to pay extra probation fees.
  • Connect account will be blocked.
  • Advising should be done by academic advisor.
  • No scholarship eligibility

error_outline Academic Dismissal Warning

Students who will be in probation for three semesters (overall CGPA remains below minimum CGPA of 2.00 for three semesters) may face academic dismissal and withdrawal from university.

warning_amber Retake Policy

What is retake?

  • A retake refers to a course you have failed and must take again.

Policy

  • For Non-credit Courses:
    - You may retake a non-credit course only once.
    - If you fail to pass it in this two attempts, you will be withdrawn from the university.
    -The passing mark for non-credit courses is 70% .
  • For Credit Courses:
    - You may retake a credit course up to two times (i.e., a total of three attempts including the first one).
    - If you fail to pass after three attempts, you will be withdrawn from the university.
    -The passing mark for credit courses is as per university grading policy.

error_outline Academic Dismissal Warning

Students who can't pass the courses within the descriped time limits may face academic dismissal and withdrawal from university.

warning_amber Attendance Requirements

Policy

  • Students are required to maintain the following threshold attendance to be eligible for final assessment/Examination:

    - 90% attendance at laboratory / studio sessions
    - 70% attendance at other classes.
  • The Head of Department, in consultation with the Registrar, will make arrangements to consider exceptions for students unable to attend classes because of major circumstances beyond their control.

error_outline Warning

Students who can't maintain the described threshold attendance may sit for final examination and get "F"/"W" grade.
