lightbulb_outline Helpful Instructions for Pre-Registration / Advising

  • While filling out your wishlist, please ensure that you are selecting courses according to the suggested course sequence provided by your department and also check the prerequisites for these courses.
  • Prepare your class routine a day before your advising session using this unofficial routine planning website. Make sure that all the data match with the official website - Connect.
  • While planning your routine:
    - Choose sections with available seats.
    - Follow the suggested course sequence to avoid future academic issues.
  • To better understand the advising/pre-registration process, please watch this video.
  • During the advising/pre-registration period, try to secure your seats as early as possible, as availability may become limited.

Process to open your library account.

lightbulb_outline Procedure to open your Library acoount:

[To open library account, you will need your gsuite email ID, Student ID Card. Make sure you have all of these things.]
- Go to Ayesha Abed Library.
- Scroll down to "My Account"and click on “Register” button.
- Fill up the form. Within 24 working hours, you will get an account activation mail from the library authority.
N.B: You won't be able to borrow any book without a library account.

lightbulb_outline Procedure to enable your OpenAthens account:


- Go to Ayesha Abed Library.
- Scroll down to "My Athens"and click on “Register” button.
- Fill up the form. Within 24 working hours, you will get an account activation mail from the library authority.
N.B: It is necessary to get access to the research articles.

Process to borrow any books / materials from library:

lightbulb_outline Procedure to borrow your desired book:


- Go to Ayesha Abed Library.
- Type the name of your desired book in search panel of the landing page.
- Check the books edition, publication year & availability status and note your books call number (e.g. 621.38223 OPP).
- Go to the self corner and you will find the range of call numbers are hung beside the self.
- Find your self using the call number and then find the book from the self uysing the call number. You may seek help from any library assitants.
- Go to the self check-out corner and scan the bar code of your Student ID card. Then scan the bar code of your desired book.
- Confirm your application and download the receipt. All Done!!!

lightbulb_outline Procedure to book your Library space:

[To book your Library space, you will need your library account. Make sure you have your library account.]
- Go to Ayesha Abed Library Space Reservation System.
- Fill up the form with valid information. Within few minutes, you will get a confirmation mail from the library authority.

How to pay your tuitions fees?

Step-1: Download your payslip

  1. 1 Go to BRACU SLMS - connect .
  2. 2 Click on the ”Payslip” tab.
  3. 3 Click on the ”view” button.
  4. 4 Click on the ”Pay Now” button for online payment [Not recommended].
  5. 5 Click on the ”Download” button for offline payment { Recommended}.

Step-2: Print out your payslip

  1. 1 Print out your payslip from BracU Campus Printer (Procedure is mentioned here) or any other way.

Step-3: Pay your fees

  1. 1 Go to your any branch of these banks:
    - Brac Bank
    - One Bank
    - Dhaka Bank
    - Dutch Bangla Bank
    - Pubali Bank
    - Southeast Bank
  2. 2Submit your payslip and tuition fees.
  3. 3Banker will process your payment.
  4. 4Collect your student copy.
  5. 5You will get a confirmation from BracU authority.

Same procedure is applicable for installment payment!!!
