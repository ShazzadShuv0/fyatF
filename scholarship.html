Understanding academic standing and financial aid requirements

star_outline Merit-Based Scholarships

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 30 Credit to avail this schlarship.
  • Must complete minimum 12 credits / 15 credits(only for Law) / / 18 credits(only for Pharmacy) both in the current and previous semester.
  • No retake & repeat courses both in the current and previous semester.
  • No "W"/"F" grade in the previous semester.

Scholarship Tiers

CGPA 4.00 100% Tuition Fee Waiver
CGPA 3.95-3.99 75% Tuition Fee Waiver
CGPA 3.90-3.94 50% Tuition Fee Waiver

CGPA 3.85-3.89 25% Tuition Fee Waiver
CGPA 3.70-3.84 10% Tuition Fee Waiver

How CGPA Affects Scholarships

  • Scholarships are awarded every semester based on cumulative GPA.
  • No need to apply for Merit Based Scholarship. It will automatically adjusted.
  • If CGPA drops below 3.70, scholarship is suspended for the following semester.
  • Students can regain scholarship by bringing CGPA back to required level.
